About TruthForge

TruthForge is a pioneering organization dedicated to ensuring that artificial intelligence serves democracy rather than undermining it. We work at the intersection of technology and governance to build systems that empower citizens and strengthen democratic institutions.

Our Focus Areas

Building democratic AI governance through three core areas that prioritize transparency, accountability, and citizen empowerment.
